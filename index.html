The Spongebob Cast!

Spongebob

The iconic 'Goofy-Goober' pro-tag of the show! He loves working the grill and is an iconic staple to everyones childhood. He's simply just a bundle of joy!

Patrick

The starfish best friend of Spongebob! What he lacks in intelligence and problem-solving he makes up for in loyalty to his best friend.

Squidward

The tentacled neighbor of Spongebob! He's absolutely miserable from working with Spongebob, but he finds enjoyment in his music and art.

Sandy

The karate-master, genius scientist squirrel from Texas! Whenever shes not busy with one of her new inventions, she can be seen helping Spongebob sort out his issues.

Mr. Krabs

The cheapskate owner of the Krusty Krab and Spongebob's boss! Known for selling his best workers soul for a measly 62 cents, and being one of the richest people in Bikini Bottom!

Plankton

The main villian of the show! He[s always hatching up a new plan to steal the Krabby Patty formula and rule Bikini Bottom. He has a deep hatred for Mr. Krabs that goes way back to their childhood.

Karen

The computer wife to Plankton. She is often the more logical side to Planktons operations, and his constantly tired of his failing to achieve the formula.

Larry

The weight-lifting lobster of Bikini Bottom. He makes appearances in various episodes especially involving Goo Lagoon (usually hanging out with Sandy), always making sure to be supportive.

Gary

Spongebobs one and only snail pet. He's one of Spongebobs dearest friends and seems to have an intelligence greater than anyone knows!
